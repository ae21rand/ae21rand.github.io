About Me

Hi, I'm Lexi - a designer, photographer, and aspiring web developer! While I'm new to web development, I'm excited to learn and create beautiful, functional designs that people enjoy using. With a background in graphic design, I have a strong eye for aesthetics and love making things look and feel just right. I believe great design should be both visually appealing and intuitive, and I'm excited to bring that philosophy into my web development journey. Thanks for stopping by, I hope you enjoy what you see!
